# Department AI Governance Rollout Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook helps each department implement Global AI Governance Solutions.

## Department Lead Responsibilities

Each department lead must ensure:

- All AI systems are reported
- Owners are assigned
- Risk tiers are reviewed
- Evidence is collected
- Vendor AI is submitted for review
- Users are trained
- Monitoring is active
- Shutdown paths are known

## 30-Day Department Actions

- Appoint department AI governance lead
- Identify all AI tools and use cases
- Report shadow AI use without penalty
- Create initial department AI inventory
- Identify high-impact use cases

## 60-Day Department Actions

- Assign owners for each AI system
- Classify risk tiers
- Begin evidence collection
- Submit vendor AI for procurement review
- Identify monitoring gaps

## 90-Day Department Actions

- Complete review for high-risk systems
- Close missing owner gaps
- Close missing shutdown gaps
- Complete required training
- Report department readiness

## 180-Day Department Actions

- Move into recurring review
- Update evidence binders
- Validate monitoring
- Run incident tabletop exercise
- Report metrics to Executive AI Governance Board
