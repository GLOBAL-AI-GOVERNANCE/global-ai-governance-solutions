# Enterprise 30/60/90/180-Day AI Governance Rollout

## First 30 Days: Establish Command

- Name executive sponsor
- Create Executive AI Governance Board
- Approve operating law
- Launch enterprise AI inventory
- Issue acceptable AI use policy
- Freeze high-risk unsanctioned deployments until reviewed
- Begin shadow AI discovery

## Days 31-60: Build Visibility and Ownership

- Complete initial AI inventory
- Assign owners
- Classify risk tiers
- Identify high-risk systems
- Identify vendor AI exposure
- Establish evidence binder structure
- Launch role-based training

## Days 61-90: Review and Control

- Complete high-risk evidence reviews
- Activate monitoring plans
- Test shutdown paths for high-risk systems
- Close vendor contract gaps
- Create first executive AI risk report
- Approve, restrict, pause, or reject systems

## Days 91-180: Institutionalize

- Move to recurring governance cadence
- Integrate AI review into procurement
- Integrate AI review into security and privacy review
- Create department scorecards
- Run AI incident tabletop
- Report quarterly to executive leadership
- Prepare audit-ready evidence package

## 180-Day Success Criteria

- 95%+ AI systems inventoried
- 100% high-risk systems assigned owners
- 100% high-risk systems have evidence binders
- 100% high-risk systems have shutdown paths
- Vendor AI contract gaps tracked
- Executive reporting cadence active
