# Employee Acceptable AI Use Training

## Audience

All employees and contractors.

## Core Rules

Employees should:

- Use only approved AI tools
- Avoid entering sensitive data into unapproved AI systems
- Verify important outputs
- Report shadow AI use
- Report harmful or incorrect outputs
- Never use AI to bypass policy, security, privacy, or legal review

## Key Message

AI can support work.

AI should not replace accountability.

No trust without verification.
