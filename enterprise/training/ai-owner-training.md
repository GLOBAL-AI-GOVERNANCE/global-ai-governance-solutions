# AI Owner Training

## Audience

Business owners, technical owners, product owners, and system owners.

## Owner Responsibilities

AI owners are responsible for:

- System inventory accuracy
- Approved use and prohibited use
- Risk tier review
- Evidence collection
- Monitoring
- User escalation
- Incident reporting
- Corrective action
- Shutdown readiness

## Owner Rule

An owner is not a symbolic name.

An owner is the person accountable for knowing what the AI system does, what can go wrong, and how it can be controlled.
