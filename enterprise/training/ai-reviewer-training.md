# AI Reviewer Training

## Audience

Security, privacy, legal, compliance, risk, audit, and procurement reviewers.

## Reviewer Responsibilities

Reviewers assess whether evidence is sufficient for the system's risk tier.

## Review Domains

- Security
- Privacy
- Legal/compliance
- Data governance
- Bias and fairness
- Human authority
- Vendor accountability
- Monitoring
- Incident response
- Shutdown path

## Reviewer Decision Options

- Approve
- Approve with restrictions
- Require more evidence
- Escalate
- Reject
- Pause or shut down
