# Executive AI Governance Training

## Audience

Executives, board members, governance leaders, and senior decision-makers.

## Training Objectives

After training, executives should be able to:

- Understand the AI estate
- Identify high-risk AI systems
- Ask for the right evidence
- Recognize ownership gaps
- Make approval, restriction, pause, or shutdown decisions
- Interpret quarterly AI risk reports

## Core Message

AI governance is not an IT issue alone.

It is an enterprise risk, strategy, legal, security, privacy, and public trust issue.

## Executive Questions

- What AI systems do we use?
- Which systems affect people, money, safety, security, rights, or trust?
- Who owns each system?
- What evidence supports deployment?
- What incidents occurred?
- What cannot be stopped?
- What requires board-level decision?
