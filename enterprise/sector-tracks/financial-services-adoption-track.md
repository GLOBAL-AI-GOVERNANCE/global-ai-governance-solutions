# Financial Services Adoption Track

## Priority Risks

- Consumer harm
- Credit and lending fairness
- Fraud and security
- Model risk management
- Data privacy
- Regulatory scrutiny
- Vendor dependency
- Explainability

## Recommended Focus

- Model inventory alignment
- Bias/fairness testing
- Audit trails
- Human review for adverse decisions
- Vendor contract controls
- Incident reporting
- Monitoring and validation
