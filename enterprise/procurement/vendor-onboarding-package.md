# AI Vendor Onboarding Package

## Purpose

No vendor AI should enter the enterprise without governance review.

## Vendor Intake Questions

- What AI capability is being provided?
- What model or system powers the capability?
- What data will be processed?
- Is customer data used for training?
- Where is data stored?
- Who are subprocessors?
- What logs are available?
- What security controls exist?
- What privacy controls exist?
- How are incidents reported?
- How are model changes disclosed?
- Can data be deleted or returned?
- What happens when the contract ends?

## Required Contract Terms

- Data use restrictions
- Data retention terms
- Data deletion/return rights
- Security requirements
- Incident notification timelines
- Audit rights
- Subprocessor disclosure
- Model update notice
- Service-level expectations
- Use restrictions
- Liability and indemnity
- Termination rights

## Procurement Decision Options

- Approved
- Approved with restrictions
- More information required
- Security review required
- Privacy review required
- Legal review required
- Rejected
