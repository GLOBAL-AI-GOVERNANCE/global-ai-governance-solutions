# AI Risk Tier Calculator

Score each dimension from 0 to 4.

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 0 | None or minimal |
| 1 | Limited |
| 2 | Meaningful |
| 3 | High |
| 4 | Critical or severe |

## Dimensions

- People/Rights Impact
- Safety/Security Impact
- Money/Operations Impact
- Autonomy Level
- Data Sensitivity
- Public Impact
- Cyber Exposure
- Shutdown Difficulty

## Suggested Tier Mapping

| Total Score | Risk Tier | Approval |
|---:|---|---|
| 0–6 | Low | Team Owner |
| 7–12 | Moderate | Business Owner |
| 13–18 | High | Executive Owner |
| 19–24 | Critical | Executive Governance Board |
| 25–32 | Frontier | Board-Level Review |

## Override Rule

Any AI system that materially affects rights, safety, national security, public trust, critical infrastructure, or high-consequence financial outcomes may be elevated regardless of total score.
