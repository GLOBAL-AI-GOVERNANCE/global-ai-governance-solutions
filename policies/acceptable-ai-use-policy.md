# Acceptable AI Use Policy

## Core Rule

AI may be used only when it is visible, owned, risk-classified, and governed.

## Acceptable Use

AI may support:

- Drafting
- Summarization
- Research assistance
- Internal productivity
- Analysis support
- Workflow assistance
- Decision support with human review

## Prohibited Use

AI may not be used to:

- Make high-impact decisions without meaningful human authority
- Process sensitive data without approval
- Bypass security controls
- Hide work from required review
- Impersonate people without authorization
- Generate deceptive, unlawful, or harmful content
- Operate outside approved scope
- Connect to systems beyond approved permissions

## Shadow AI

Unapproved AI use must be reported, inventoried, classified, and either governed or removed.
