# Human Authority Policy

## Core Rule

No high-impact AI may operate without meaningful, timely human authority.

## High-Impact Systems

High-impact systems include systems that affect:

- Rights
- Safety
- Security
- Money
- Employment
- Benefits
- Legal outcomes
- Public trust
- Mission-critical operations

## Minimum Human Authority

Human authority must include:

- Review
- Override
- Correction
- Escalation
- Shutdown capability

## Meaningful Human Review

Human review is meaningful only when the human reviewer has:

- Sufficient context
- Sufficient time
- Ability to question the output
- Ability to override the output
- Protection from automation bias
- Clear accountability

## Prohibited Practice

Rubber-stamp review does not count as human authority.
