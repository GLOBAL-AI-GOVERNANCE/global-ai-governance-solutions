# Doctrine

AI is not the enemy.  
**Unowned, unseen, untested, unaccountable AI is the enemy.**

This framework turns chaos into aligned order through:

- Visibility
- Ownership
- Evidence
- Control
- Accountability

## Operating Law

No AI system moves faster than ownership, evidence, authority, and control.

## Core Belief

AI governance should not be theater.

It should make real systems visible, owned, classified, tested, governed, monitored, corrected, and stoppable.

## The Enemy

The enemy is:

- Unowned AI
- Unseen AI
- Untested AI
- Unaccountable AI
- Autonomous power without authority
- Vendor systems without contractual accountability
- Decision systems without appeal or correction
- Frontier capabilities without a shutdown path

## The Solution

The solution is disciplined command.

Not panic.  
Not hype.  
Not delay for delay's sake.

Governance.

Visible. Accountable. Governable.
