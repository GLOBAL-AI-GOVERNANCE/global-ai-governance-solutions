# Example: Low-Risk Chatbot

## System

Internal FAQ chatbot for non-sensitive employee policy questions.

## Risk Tier

Low

## Approved Use

- Summarize public internal policy documents
- Help employees find relevant links
- Provide non-binding guidance

## Prohibited Use

- Legal advice
- HR decisions
- Medical advice
- Processing sensitive personal data
- Final policy interpretation

## Required Controls

- Named owner
- Inventory entry
- Approved data source list
- User disclaimer
- Feedback channel
- Monitoring for incorrect answers
- Shutdown path

## Approval Decision

Approved for internal pilot with monitoring.
