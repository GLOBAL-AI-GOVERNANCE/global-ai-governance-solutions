# Example: High-Impact Decision System

## System

AI-assisted eligibility screening tool for financial assistance.

## Risk Tier

High

## Why High

The system may affect money, access to services, public trust, and individual outcomes.

## Approved Use

- Triage applications for human review
- Identify missing documentation
- Support consistency checks

## Prohibited Use

- Final denial without human review
- Automated eligibility rejection
- Use of unapproved personal data
- Black-box scoring without explanation

## Required Controls

- Human authority
- Appeal and correction process
- Audit trail
- Bias testing
- Security review
- Privacy review
- Legal review
- Monitoring
- Incident response
- Shutdown path

## Approval Decision

Pilot only. Final decisions remain with trained human reviewers.
